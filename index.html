About

Tanner is a sophomore at George Mason Univeristy studying Computer Science.

They love getting their hands dirty with random scraps of code and geometry.

They have too many projects and too little time.

They want to share them with you.

Projects

A simple command-line calculator I wrote in an hour or two one weekend to experiment with lexical parsing. Respects full order of operations. A few pretty shaders. Er, one pretty shader. But it does a lot! I used this shader to create the banner for this webpage. A little Lua script I made to parse some spreadsheet data that we never really used in the end. Features flexible scripting via Crunchfile. A raycaster I wrote using nothing but Lua and LOVE2D over the course of 2 weeks for #LOWREZJAM2016.
